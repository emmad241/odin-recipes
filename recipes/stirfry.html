Stir Fry

Lovely vegan stirfry

This lovely vegan noodle recipe will spice up your life.

This recipe is delicious, nutritious, and cruelty-free!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Tofu
  • Peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the noodle
  2. Chop the vegetables
  3. Heat the frying pan
  4. Stirfry the veg
  5. Add the noodles and stir
  6. Serve it up!
