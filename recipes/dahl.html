Dhal

Lovely vegan dhal

This dhalicious vegan dahl recipe was given to me by my grandmother. This recipe will make your mouth water and leave you wanting more!

Ingredients

  • 200g lentils
  • 2 onions
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Mushrooms
  • Cumin
  • Tumeric

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Heat the frying pan
  3. Fry the veg
  4. Add lentils, tomatoes, water
  5. Serve it up!
