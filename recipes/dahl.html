Lovely vegan dahl

This lovely vegan dahl recipe will spice up your life.

This recipe is delicious, nutritious, and cruelty-free!

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Cumin
  • Tumeric

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Heat the frying pan
  3. Fry the veg
  4. Add lentils, tomatoes, water
  5. Serve it up!
